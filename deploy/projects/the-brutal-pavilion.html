THE BRUTAL PAVILION

LocationBerlin, Germany
Year2022
TypologyCultural

An exhibition space exploring weight and levitation through brutalist forms.

Concept

Challenging the perception of weight, this pavilion appears to float above its minimal glass casing. The heavily textured concrete ceiling presses downward, yet the expansive ground floor retains an ephemeral lightness, blurring the boundary between structure and atmosphere.
